Rules

1 Jab
2 Cross
3 Lead hook
4 Rear hook
5 Lead uppercut
6 Rear uppercut

Before start of each round, memorize the combination of moves that is displayed.

Each punch corresponds to a number on your keyboard.

During the fight, perform the combination you have just learned before.

If you succeed in your attack, you win the round.

There are a total of 5 rounds, win as many as possible to become the new champion.
